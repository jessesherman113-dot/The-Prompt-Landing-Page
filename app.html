Chain-of-Thought for Interview Prep
Don't just practice interview answers — understand why strong answers work. This prompt forces the AI to show its reasoning before giving you the model answer.
"Think step by step. I'm prepping for [role]. Question: '[question]'. Before answering: (1) What is the interviewer actually evaluating? (2) What does a weak answer look like? Then give me a strong STAR answer using my background: [your context]."

01 // RESUME & APPLICATIONS
Full Resume Audit ESSENTIAL
"Act as a senior recruiter. Review my resume for [target role]. Flag weak verbs, missing metrics, and ATS keywords. Return a prioritized list of fixes ordered by impact. Be direct." [Paste your resume]
Bullet Transformer ESSENTIAL
"Rewrite this bullet using the STAR framework with a strong verb and quantified impact. Keep under 20 words. Give 2 versions — conservative and bold. Original: [your bullet]"
Job Description Decoder TARGETING
"Analyze this JD. Return: (1) must-have skills, (2) nice-to-haves, (3) red flags, (4) core problem this role solves, (5) top 3 ATS keywords I need. [Paste JD]"
Tailored Cover Letter TARGETING
"Write a 3-paragraph cover letter for [role] at [company]. My background: [summary]. Match to: [key JD requirements]. Open with a hook, not 'I am applying.' Tone: direct, confident, human."
02 // LINKEDIN & PERSONAL BRAND
LinkedIn Headline Generator ESSENTIAL
"Generate 5 LinkedIn headlines for me. I am a [background] targeting [role] in [industry]. Top skills: [list 3]. Formula: [Role] | [Specialty] | [Value]. Make each distinct."
Personal Brand Statement BRAND
"Write my personal brand statement. I help [audience] who struggle with [challenge] using [skill 1] and [skill 2] to deliver [outcome]. Give 3 versions: LinkedIn, verbal intro, email signature."
03 // INTERVIEW PREPARATION
Mock Interviewer ESSENTIAL
"You are a tough senior interviewer for [role]. Ask me 5 behavioral questions one at a time. After each answer, give critical specific feedback. Do not give all 5 at once. Start with question 1."
STAR Story Bank ESSENTIAL
"Generate 6 STAR interview stories from my background covering: leadership, conflict, failure, impact, change, collaboration. Each under 200 words with a specific result. My background: [3-5 sentences]"
Chain-of-Thought Prep NEW
"Think step by step. I'm prepping for [role]. Question: '[question]'. First: (1) what is the interviewer evaluating, (2) what does a weak answer look like. Then give a strong STAR answer using: [your background]."
04 // OFFER & NEGOTIATION
Market Rate Research ESSENTIAL
"I'm negotiating salary for [role] in [city] with [X] years experience. Give me: (1) realistic 2026 comp range with base/bonus/equity, (2) what pushes to top of range, (3) data sources to check, (4) one-sentence opening to anchor my number."
Counter-Offer Email ESSENTIAL
"Write a counter-offer email. Role: [role]. Offered: $[X]. Target: $[Y]. Justification: [market rate/experience]. Open with enthusiasm, state ask clearly, leave room for other components, close warmly."
